General Information

Online Play is a feature in Project_Gamblitz allowing mod users to play online with or against other users.

Project_Gamblitz server

The mod uses a LAN server in order to handle the Online Play status view, kicks, stats and other stuff. This information is sent by your console in the background and can be seen in the Project_Gamblitz Discord Server.

"Gamblitz" Console ID

In order to tell your console appart from others, a unique console ID is used through all Project_Gamblitz Server communications. This ID consists of 16 symbols from 0 to 9 and A to F. The ID is a hash derived from a series of other console unique IDs tied to your NAND chip (since it is a hash, it cannot be used in reverse to obtain your private IDs). You can treat the "Gamblitz" Console ID as your "friend code" specific for the mod.

You have the option to get your "Gamblitz" Console ID on the first boot with the mod, if you choose to not create it, you won't be able to play Online with people that have their ID.

Online Play Rules

Online Play has a series of rules so everybody can play safely and enjoy the game:

  1. Names containing profanity, insults or any other kind offensive, inappropiate or annoying words are not allowed.
    • This also includes impersonation of other users.
  2. Repeatedly using exploits that are disruptive or give you advantage over other players is not allowed. If you find such exploit please report it in the Project_Gamblitz server. For example:
    • Using hacks or cheats (instant perma-ban).
    • Using any sort of external tool that can interfere with the gameplay (instant perma-ban).
    • Using unintended glitches that give advantage.
    • Teaming with other users to get strategical advantage, unless fomented by the current game mode or ongoing event.
  3. Trolling is not allowed unless it is fomented by the current game mode or gives you a strategical advantage. For example:
    • Do not have a toxic attitude against other players.
If you are found breaking any of this rules you will be given a warning, be temporarily banned from Online Play. While permanent bans are rare, repeated severe offenses will cause them.

Privacy Notice about Telemety

All the activity and requests your console does related to Online Play is logged. This is done so we can properly moderate the Online Play. The logged contents include your "Gamblitz" Console ID and Mii/Account name. By acepting the agreement on the first time boot, you agree that the Staff members can see this information (which will be always kept private).
