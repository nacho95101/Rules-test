Sub weapons
Special weapons

Splat Bomb

A bomb that explodes a short time after it lands.
It inks the area around it when it explodes and can even take out opponents.

Suction Bomb

A bomb that attaches to the ground and walls.
They take longer to explode than the Splat Bombs and spread ink farther.

Burst Bomb

A bomb that explodes as soon as it touches something.
It's a small, weak explosion, but they don't consume much ink so you can throw them one after another.

Curling Bomb

A bomb that moves in a straight line inking the ground before it explodes.
It also bounces off walls!
You can adjust the length of time until detonation by holding down .

Autobomb

A bomb that automatically seeks out opponents.
It will search for opponents in the area that you throw it, and if it finds one, it will automatically start walking toward them.
When it approaches an opponent, it stops and explodes.

Ink Mine

A trap that you place on the ground. When an opponent gets close, it activates, marking that opponent.
It'll also activate if inked by an opponent.
You can place two at once.

Toxic Mist

A bottle that sprays the fluid of a certain creature in mist form.
Opponents caught in the mist move slower and their ink is slightly reduced.
The longer you're in the mist, the stronger the effect becomes.

Point Sensor

A device that marks opponents nearby where it is thrown.
It reveals the opponents' location to your teammates.

Splash Wall

A device that erects a wall of ink in front of you that blocks enemy intrusion and attacks.
Attacks from enemies will speed up the time it takes for the wall to come down.
You can only place one at a time.

Sprinkler

A device that attaches to the ground or to walls and sprays ink.
It operates at full power right when you place it but gradually gets weaker.
You can only place one at a time.

Squid Beakon

A device that emits a signal that squids can detect.
You can Super Jump to a teammate's Squid Beakon by selecting it and pressing . Up to three beakons can be set at any given time, and although they're usually only good for one jump, they will last two jumps if used by players who do not have Squid Beakons equipped.

Fizzy Bomb

A bomb loaded with special carbonated ink.
Hold  to build power, then unleash up to three explosions.
Shake the controller, move the stick or mash the buttons to build power quicker!

Torpedo

A sub weapon that flies toward your enemies.
When thrown near an enemy, it changes shape and targets the foe, exploding on impact and splatting ink everywhere.
You can shoot down enemy torpedoes.

Tenta Missiles

If you press , this will fire missiles at all enemies that you tag with your cursor.
The more enemies you tag, the more effective it is.

Sting Ray

Hold down  to fire a high-pressure jet of ink that can blast through walls.
You can't change direction quickly while firing this.
When not firing, you can see the location of opponents on the other side of walls.

Inkjet

This launches you into the air by spraying out ink, allowing you to attack opponents with the attached launcher.
You can fly higher temporarily by pressing .

Splashdown

You charge up power while floating upward, then slam down to the ground, attacking the surrounding area.
The range of the attack is wider if used from an elevated area.
It can also be used during a Super Jump!

Ink Armor

Grants each member of your team armor that will completely nullify the damage from a single attack.
Works on all team members regardless of proximity.

Bomb Launchers

These launch the included bombs without consuming any of your ink.
The longer you hold , the farther you'll throw the bombs.

Ink Storm

Throw this device to generate a rain cloud of ink.
The cloud gradually moves away from where the device was thrown.

Baller

Enter a ball that protects you from a certain amount of damage and move around.
If you hold down  to charge up power or let a certain amount of time pass, the ball will explode, freeing you and attacking the surrounding area.

Bubble Blower

This fires a maximum of three bubbles that, when inked in your teammates' color, burst and attack the surrounding area.
If inked in your opponents' color, they shrivel up.

Booyah Bomb

Funnel power into an explosive ink ball and launch it at the enemy.
By sending and receiving "Booyahs" from your team, you can build power more quickly.
Teammates who send Booyahs will be rewarded by having their special gauge filled a little.

Ultra Stamp

Attack with an enormous hammer.
Press and hold  to advance while striking with the hammer.
If you press and release , you'll throw the hammer far into the distance to attack, and the special will end.
