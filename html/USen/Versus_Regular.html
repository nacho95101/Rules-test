Regular Battle

In a Regular Battle, teams have three minutes to cover the ground with ink. Whichever team inks the most turf wins!
Get inking and try to claim as much turf as possible for your team!

Special Weapons

The more turf you ink, the more your special gauge will fill.
When it's completely full, unleash your special with !
Your special gauge drops if you're splatted, so watch out! Make sure to unleash its fury when it will be most effective.

Turf Map

The freshest players all know to check the Turf Map with .
Use it to get an overview of each team's inked turf at a glance.
Don't forget, you can keep playing while looking at the map too!

Super Jump

With the Turf Map open, select an ally with , and Super Jump to their location using !
Take a moment to assess the Turf Map before choosing a landing point. You don't want to Super Jump right into the enemy's clutches!
You can also use  to choose which ally to Super Jump to.

Points & Cash

Make sure you stay in a battle until the very end to earn cash and points.
As you earn points and increase your level, shops will make new weapons available to you!
If you want to be fresh, fighting battles with a wide array of weaponry is the way to go.
