How to play Salmon Run
Collect Golden Eggs and get yourself a bonus!
  • Defeat the Boss Salmonid Find a Golden Egg!
  • Put the Golden Egg into the egg basket The Golden Egg is yours!
  • Reach your Golden Egg quota before time runs out! Wave cleared!
  • Clear all three waves Get points!
  • Exchange points at the counter in the square Get that bonus!
