Please keep the following rules in mind.
Posts that break these rules may
not be displayed to other users.

Personal information

Do not include any information that can be used to identify you or someone else personally, such as your name (or someone else's name), e-mail address, home address, or telephone number.

Content that might make others uncomfortable

Posted drawings may be visible to other users.
Do not include words or images in your posts that may upset other users.

Inappropriate content

Do not post any commercial or marketing content, content that depicts violence, or content that violates public morality.

Misreporting

Do not deliberately report content that does not violate the rules.

Copyright infringement

Be careful not to post content that infringes upon the intellectual property rights of others.

How do I post my image to the Internet?

You can post your image to social media if you select "Post" on the drawing screen while connected to the Internet.
◆
A social media account is required.

How can I make Splatfest posts?

Once a Splatfest theme is announced, you can make posts after you visit the Pledge Box in the plaza and pick a team!
You won't be able to make Splatfest posts if you haven't chosen a team to support.

I've found an image that violates the rules. What should I do?

Please report any inappropriate content that you find.

How do I report something?

While in the square, select some graffiti on a wall or another surface, and use  to bring up the reporting screen. Then select the reason for reporting the image and send the report.

Can I prevent images from being displayed in the square?

While there's no way to prevent images from displaying altogether, you can opt for only preset images created by Nintendo to be displayed.
From the Options tab in the Menu, change Post Display to OFF.
